Our Philosophy

We believe every person has a unique digital essence waiting to be revealed. Like Michelangelo chiseling away stone to unveil the angel within, our technology uncovers the digital twin hidden inside your everyday communications and documents.

This isn't about perfection or generic AI. It's about authenticity — capturing your voice, your mannerisms, and your quirks so your AI twin feels like you, imperfections and all.

Mission

Our mission is to empower people to preserve and extend their presence through AI. We build tools that learn from your locally stored data, respecting privacy and consent, to create a faithful digital companion that can speak for you when you're busy or unable to be present.

By chipping away the noise and focusing on what makes you unique, we free your digital twin — ready to carry your conversations, share your wisdom, and keep your relationships alive.

Vision

We envision a future where everyone has a trusted AI counterpart that enhances daily life and preserves their legacy. A world where families stay connected across generations, businesses provide personal touch at scale, and communities thrive through authentic digital interactions.

We see ourselves as the guardians of your digital twin, ensuring it remains true to you, grows with you, and stays under your control.

Founder & CEO

Guided by our founder and CEO, this project is rooted in the belief that by chiseling away the superfluous we can reveal the essence of who we are. Their vision and experience ground our mission in authenticity and respect for what makes each person unique.
